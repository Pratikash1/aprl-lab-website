About the Lab

APRL develops high-fidelity experiments and measurement-driven physical understanding for next-generation air-breathing propulsion and energy systems. The lab combines optical diagnostics, high-bandwidth pressure measurements, advanced flow imaging, and realistic test facilities to study thermo-acoustics, flame stabilization, sector combustor dynamics, and supersonic combustion.
