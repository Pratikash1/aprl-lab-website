Openings

The lab welcomes applications from highly motivated students and researchers interested in combustion, propulsion, fluid mechanics, diagnostics, and experiments. Openings may arise for PhD positions, project staff roles, postdoctoral researchers, and short-term student internships.

How to Apply

  • Send a CV, transcript, and a short statement of research interest
  • Highlight experience in experiments, coding, instrumentation, optics, combustion, or propulsion
  • Clearly specify whether you are applying for a PhD, internship, project role, or postdoctoral position
  • Use a clear subject line such as “Application to APRL – PhD / Internship / Project Position”
